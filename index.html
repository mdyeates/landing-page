The Four Greatest Cycling Destinations In The World
Looking for a change of scenery on your bicycle? Discover one of these four awe-inspiring destinations...
Choose between: Girona, Mallorca, Southern France and Slovenia

Girona really does have everything: quiet roads, varied terrain, quality restaurants, a medieval city centre, cafès & a buzzing cycling culture

Mallorca offers long and steady climbs, tough enough to be a challenge and forgiving enough to give you time to enjoy the ride

Mont Ventoux can be ridden from 3 different start points, Malaucène to the Northwest, Sault to the East and the traditional and oldest ascent from Bedoin to the South

The dynamic landscapes of Slovenia, with their countless natural beauties, are ideal for a biking holiday
